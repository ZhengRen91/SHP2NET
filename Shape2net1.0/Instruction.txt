This small program convert the shapefile to the pajek .net format based on the connectivity of the line features.
In order to run this program on your PC, you should have ArcObjects 10.2 SDK for Microsoft .NET installed in your PC, which can be found at the first step when you install the ArcMap. 
How to do: just choose your shapefile at first textbox, and choose the output location at the second textbox and click 'shape to net' button.
